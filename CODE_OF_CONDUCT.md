# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible
disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual
identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and
healthy community.

## Our Standards

We expect everyone to be respectful, considerate, and collaborative:

- Be welcoming and kind; assume good intent and give constructive feedback.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Accept responsibility, apologize to those affected by our mistakes, and learn from them.
- Focus on what is best for the overall community.

Behavior that harasses, demeans, or excludes others is not tolerated.

## Adoption

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1**. The full text — including the detailed
standards and enforcement guidelines — is available at
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.

## Enforcement

Concerns about unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer at
**codyamaughan@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and
the maintainer will respect the privacy and security of the reporter. The full enforcement guidelines
are in the Contributor Covenant 2.1 linked above.
